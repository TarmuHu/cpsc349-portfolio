Overview

A real-time hand gesture recognition game that lets users play rock, paper, or scissors using their hands in front of a webcam. The computer detects gestures and responds instantly with a countdown and result display.

Features

  • Real-time hand tracking using MediaPipe
  • Gesture classification (Rock, Paper, Scissors and Unknown)
  • Countdown animation and show the result
  • Single-player vs computer

Technologies Used

Python, OpenCV, MediaPipe

Challenges

Ensuring stable detection under different lighting conditions, and syncing gesture recognition with visual feedback like the countdown and result message.
